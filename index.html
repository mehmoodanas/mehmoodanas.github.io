Recent projects.

Bachelor's Thesis · Featured 2024 – 2025

AI in Customer Service Automation

A transformer-based conversational AI for CRM systems. DistilBERT fine-tuned for intent classification on a curated customer-support corpus, and Sentence-BERT for semantic similarity retrieval. Two-service architecture: a Python / Flask ML inference micro-service paired with a Java / Spring Boot dialogue backend and an HTML/JS front-end over REST. Comparative evaluation of rule-based vs. DistilBERT vs. Sentence-BERT — benchmarking accuracy, confidence and latency to quantify the interpretability-vs-precision trade-off.
